name: Kalki Koechlin, introduction: Kalki Koechlin ( (listen); born 10 January 1982) is an Indian-born French actress and writer. Known for her unconventional body of work in the Indian film industry, she is the recipient of such accolades as a National Film Award, a Filmfare Award, and two Screen Awards. Although a French citizen, she has been brought up and lived most of her life in India.
