name: Evelyn Waugh, introduction: Arthur Evelyn St. John Waugh (; 28 October 1903 ¨C 10 April 1966) was an English writer of novels, biographies, and travel books; he was also a prolific journalist and book reviewer. His most famous works include the early satires Decline and Fall (1928) and A Handful of Dust (1934), the novel Brideshead Revisited (1945), and the Second World War trilogy Sword of Honour (1952¨C1961). He is recognised as one of the great prose stylists of the English language in the 20th century.
